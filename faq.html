What is the Deltron Training & Placement program?
The Deltron Training & Placement program is a comprehensive career development program designed to provide individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in today's job market. The program offers on-job training with the industry exposure that you need for the corporate world.
What does the program offer?
The program offers training and placement in a variety of fields. Some of them include Digital Marketing, Business Development, Recruitment, Sales, Voice Process, Human Resources, IT Sales, Content Writing, and more.
How long is the program?
The length of the program is 3 months.
How much does the program cost?
The cost of the Training and Internship at Deltron is minimal. If you apply for 4- weeks of Internship with the company, you have to pay a minimal fee of 2000 rupees. On the other hand, the professional training of 3 months costs 15000 rupees.
How can I apply for the program?
To apply for the program, you can give us your information here: (Contact Us Page Link). You can even give us a call at 7303360679 and we will get back to you at the earliest.
Will the program guarantee me a job?
We are offering a 100% Placement Guarantee once you complete the program. Therefore, fret not because we will not only handle the training but make sure you are placed in the best company suitable as per your profile.
